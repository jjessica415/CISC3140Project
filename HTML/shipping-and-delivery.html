SHIPPING & DELIVERY

All prices are in USD (United States Dollar). Shipping is available to the US, Canada, and internationally EXCEPT the UK, EU, and all countries that are listed under international mail service suspensions

The current processing time for all orders is 2-3 weeks.

Do note that I include personalised notes in the packaging using the name provided by the customer during checkout. This is the name that will be on the shipment label.
  • If you plan to gift your order to someone else, do let me know in the checkout notes and I will either replace or exclude names in the packaging.
  • If you would like me to address you by a preferred name that differs from the name on the shipment label, do let me know in the checkout notes as well. Stay safe!


Shipping details:


All orders will be shipped with United States Postal Service (Local/International) and will come with a tracking number.
