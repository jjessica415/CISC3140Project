ABOUT

CLOUDYCAPS is a part-time artist and nap enthusiast from Toronto, Canada. She started posting her art on Instagram in 2013 and has been drawing ever since! Her illustrations are inspired by every day life: fresh green plants, cute little animals, and magical starry skies.

Working and designing together with her amazing partner, she wants to share her prints, stickers, and enamel pins with the world! Our goal is to brighten your day and bring you happiness with soft and simple art~

We're also super pleased and grateful to be featuring a jeweler with over 40 years of crafting experience! As the founder/owner of a jewelry store, he makes wonderful custom creations with his magical hands every day. He brings our ideas for magical jewelry to life!
