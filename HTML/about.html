ABOUT

CLOUDYCAPS is a keyboard enthusiast based in the United States. She first learned of making artisan keycaps back in 2020, a few months after the COVID-19 quarantine was issued. What started off as a modest pastime, gradually transformed into a deeper passion for her. It wasn't until early January of 2023 when she decided to document her journey by posting on Instagram, where shes been actively engaging with other keycap creators and keyboard enthusiasts.

Her works draw inspiration from everyday life as well as other keycap creators on the platform. Together with her partner, they work together to design Working and designing together with her amazing partner, she wants to share her prints, stickers, and enamel pins with the world! Our goal is to brighten your day and bring you happiness with soft and simple art~

We're also super pleased and grateful to be featuring a jeweler with over 40 years of crafting experience! As the founder/owner of a jewelry store, he makes wonderful custom creations with his magical hands every day. He brings our ideas for magical jewelry to life!
